Fundraising for the needs of the Ukrainian soldiers

Our Defenders are strong and have indomitable spirit. Today, the whole world admires their heroism. However, they are in dire need of medicine and basic military equipment (bulletproof vests, helmets, etc.) to protect their Motherland.

Charitable Foundation
“Our Galycia”

Charitable Organization “Our Galycia” has been operating since 2017. Until recently, the key areas of our activity were the restoration and creation of historical and cultural heritage, support for national and cultural development programs. However, after the Russian invasion, we, like most organizations in the country, focused all our efforts on helping the Ukrainian army.
